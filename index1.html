About Me

Hello! I'm Aniket Roy, a passionate Graphic and Motion Graphics Designer based in Kolkata, India. With over 7 years of professional experience, I specialize in creating visually compelling designs that communicate brand messages effectively.

My journey in design began when I was studying Visual Arts at the University of Delhi. I was fascinated by how design could influence emotions and behaviors, and this curiosity turned into a lifelong passion.

I believe in the power of storytelling through design. Whether it's a brand identity, an animated explainer video, or a social media campaign, I approach each project with creativity, strategy, and attention to detail.
